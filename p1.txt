Dear Shri Deepak Yadav ji,  

In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 80(1)(a) and 80(3) of the Constitution of India, the Honourable President of India has been pleased to nominate you as a Member of the Rajya Sabha (Council of States) in recognition of your distinguished contribution to social service and the national interest.  

Your appointment shall take effect immediately and will be subject to the provisions of the Constitution of India and the Rules of Parliament. You are expected to discharge your duties with impartiality and sincerity.

Wishing you all the very best for your new responsibilities.
