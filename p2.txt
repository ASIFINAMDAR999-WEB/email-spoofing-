Dear Sir/Madam,

I am directed to convey that the Hon’ble President of India, in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 239 of the Constitution of India, has been pleased to appoint Dr. K. P. Ramalingam, Ex-Member of Parliament, as the Lieutenant Governor of the Union Territory of Puducherry.

This appointment shall take effect from 1 May 2025.

The official Order in this regard has been issued separately for necessary information and action.

Yours sincerely,
Ms. Deepti Umashankar
Secretary to the President
President’s Secretariat
Rashtrapati Bhavan, New Delhi
