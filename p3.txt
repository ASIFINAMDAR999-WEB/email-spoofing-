Dear Sir/Madam,

I am directed to inform you that, in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 239 of the Constitution of India, the Hon’ble President of India has appointed Bishop Dr. Godfrey Washington Noble, DD as the Lieutenant Governor of the Union Territory of Puducherry. This appointment shall take effect from 05 May 2025.

The official Order in this regard has been issued separately for your information and necessary action. You are requested to take the necessary measures to facilitate Bishop Dr. Noble’s assumption of office on the aforementioned date.

Yours sincerely,

Ms. Deepti Umashankar
Secretary to the President
President’s Secretariat
Rashtrapati Bhavan, New Delhi
